Most businesses don't have a software problem. They have a systems problem.

Growth engineering isn't just building apps. It's the intersection of three things most SMEs treat as separate:

1. Software that automates the repetitive
2. Data that tells you where to point it
3. Strategy that connects both to revenue

When these three move together, you stop guessing and start measuring.

We've seen a 15-person logistics company cut order processing time by 60% — not because they built a fancy app, but because they connected their CRM to their invoicing and removed 4 manual steps in the middle.

That's the work. Not just code. Connected systems that produce measurable outcomes.
