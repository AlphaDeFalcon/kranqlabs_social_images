## AI Image Prompts (Replicate — Ideogram v2)

### Thu Jun 4 — "How We Think About Building Software"
**Concept:** Precision, measurement, building with intent. Not code on a screen — the moment before creation where direction is set.

**Prompt:**
A minimalist architectural drafting table at golden hour, warm African sunlight streaming across a clean workspace. On the table: a single precision compass resting on a white blueprint, one sharp pencil, a metal ruler catching the light. The blueprint shows clean geometric lines suggesting interconnected systems, not a building. Shallow depth of field, the background softly blurred. Premium, calm, strategic atmosphere. No screens, no code, no people visible. Photorealistic, editorial photography style, 85mm lens equivalent.

**Negative:** text, letters, words, numbers on the blueprint, people, laptops, phones, screens, cluttered desk, coffee cups, messy, dark, gloomy

**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (1080x1080)

### Sat Jun 6 — "Why Most SME Websites Don't Convert"
**Concept:** A storefront metaphor — one clearly inviting, one confusing. The difference between clarity and clutter in physical form.

**Prompt:**
Split composition photograph. Left side: a beautifully minimalist boutique storefront with one clean window display, single elegant product on a pedestal, one clear entrance, warm inviting light spilling out. Right side: a cluttered shop with three competing signs, window covered in posters, no clear entrance, confusing. The lighting transitions from warm and focused on the left to harsh and scattered on the right. Editorial photography, architectural detail, commercial street scene. African urban context, Accra-style modern commercial building. No people visible. No text or letters on any signage — the difference communicates purely through design, light, and clarity.

**Negative:** text, letters, words, logos, branding on storefronts, people, stock photography look, cartoon, illustration

**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (1080x1080)

### Sun Jun 7 — "The Systems Mindset"
**Concept:** Interconnected parts forming a harmonious whole. Systems as beauty, not bureaucracy.

**Prompt:**
Overhead flat lay of an intricate but elegant mechanical clockwork partially assembled on a rich dark walnut surface. Brass gears of varying sizes interconnected, some larger wheels driving smaller ones, the composition radiating outward from a central mechanism. Warm directional light from the side creating long soft shadows. The arrangement feels intentional and calm — every piece has a place. Not steampunk or vintage — modern, minimal, precision-engineered. The gears suggest interconnected systems working in harmony. No clock face, no numbers, no text. Editorial product photography, high-end watchmaker aesthetic, macro detail shot.

**Negative:** text, numbers, clock face, hands, steampunk, rusty, dirty, messy, dark background, people, hands

**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (1080x1080)
